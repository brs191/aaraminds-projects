# 01 · Product

Product artifacts — the **what** and the **why**.

## What's here

- `Aara_Teams_Aggregator_PRD.docx` — Product Requirements Document, v0.2
- `Aara_Decisions_Needed.docx` — tracking memo for the 12 open questions from PRD §15. One row per decision: owner, due date, option-space, recommendation, what it blocks, status. Owned by Raja, reviewed Mondays until everything closes.
- `Aara_Stakeholder_Review_Brief.pptx` — 8-slide deck for the v0.2 PRD review meeting. Problem, solution, architecture at a glance, key choices, phased rollout, decisions needed by stakeholder group, asks for the meeting, timeline.

## What goes here over time

- Updated PRD versions (keep dated copies in `../99-archive/` when superseded)
- User research notes, interview transcripts, survey results
- Product decisions log (lightweight: decision · context · alternatives · date)
- Competitive briefs
- Feature one-pagers
- Metrics review write-ups

## Conventions

- **Versioning:** bump the version in the PRD's metadata table; move the old `.docx` to `../99-archive/` with a date suffix (e.g., `Aara_Teams_Aggregator_PRD_2026-05-26.docx`)
- **Naming:** `Aara_<artifact>_<short-descriptor>.{docx,md,pdf}`
- **Source of truth:** the latest `.docx` in this folder is canonical
