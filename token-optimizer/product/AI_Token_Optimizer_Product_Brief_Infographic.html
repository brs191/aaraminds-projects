The decision gate

Thresholds are starting points — calibrate to AITO's economics before the spike
Green Build the narrow product
Median token reduction ≥ 25% · quality regression ≤ 5% of A/B pairs · latency < 300 ms p95 · net savings positive · niche wanted as a product
Amber Keep as internal tooling
Savings real (≥ 15%) but the niche is not wanted as a product, or quality and latency are acceptable but not great
Red Adopt off-the-shelf, or shelve
Reduction < 15%, or measurable quality regression on normal coding prompts, or unacceptable latency
